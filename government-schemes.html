Government Schemes

Government Health Schemes at Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, we believe that quality healthcare should be within reach for every family in Gadag and neighbouring regions. To reduce the financial burden of medical treatment, our hospital extends support through recognised government health schemes. With dedicated guidance from our team, patients can avail affordable, reliable care without worrying about expenses.

Currently, Sangameshwar Hospital is empanelled under two major schemes:

Yeshasvini Health Insurance Scheme in Gadag

The Yeshasvini Co-operative Farmers Health Care Scheme is one of Karnataka’s flagship healthcare initiatives, designed to provide affordable treatment to members of co-operative societies.

Yeshasvini Health Insurance Scheme
Key Benefits for Patients
  • Coverage for more than 800 surgical procedures at network hospitals.
  • Substantial reduction in treatment and surgical costs.
  • Access to multispeciality care, including general surgery, orthopaedics, cardiology, and more.
Eligibility
  • Must be a member of a co-operative society in Karnataka for at least 3 months.
  • Applicable to both rural and urban co-operative society members.
Required Documents
  • Yeshasvini Health Card / Membership ID.
  • Aadhaar Card.
  • Proof of co-operative society membership.
How Sangameshwar Hospital Helps

Our hospital’s reception desk guides patients through every step from verifying eligibility to processing claims. We ensure a smooth, transparent process so patients can focus on recovery while we take care of the paperwork.

Ayushman Bharat Arogya Karnataka (AB-ARK) Scheme at Sangameshwar Hospital

The AB-ARK scheme combines the benefits of the central government’s Ayushman Bharat Yojana with the Karnataka government’s Arogya Karnataka scheme. It aims to provide cashless healthcare to economically weaker sections of society.

Ayushman Bharat Arogya Karnataka (AB-ARK)
Key Benefits for Patients
  • Coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care.
  • Wide range of treatments across specialities, including emergency care, surgeries, and chronic disease management.
  • Cashless treatment at Sangameshwar Hospital and other empanelled centres.
Eligibility
  • Families listed under the Socio-Economic Caste Census (SECC) database.
  • Priority household cardholders (BPL, Antyodaya Anna Yojana).
  • Certain categories of APL families as notified by the Karnataka government.
Required Documents
  • Aadhaar Card of all family members.
  • Ration Card (BPL/APL as applicable).
  • Ayushman Bharat/AB-ARK Health Card (if already issued).
How Sangameshwar Hospital Helps
  • Document verification and scheme registration.
  • Cashless admission and discharge process.
  • Liaison with government portals to ensure benefits are processed without delay.

With us, patients can receive timely and affordable care without worrying about financial strain.

Future Prospects

At Sangameshwar Hospital, we are committed to strengthening patient access through quality certifications and partnerships. We are working towards NABH accreditation and planning collaborations with major insurance providers for cashless treatment. In the near future, patients will also be able to benefit from schemes such as the ESI scheme and Arogya Bhagya Yojane, further expanding affordable healthcare options.
