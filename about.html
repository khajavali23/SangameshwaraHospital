About Us

About us

A Complete Shelter for Every Medical Need

Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital is a 100-bed multispeciality hospital based in Gadag, Karnataka. The hospital carries forward the legacy of Dr K.B. Dhannur, who served the people of Naregal with dedication and compassion for over five decades. His name became synonymous with trust, integrity, and patient-first care in the region.

Continuing this legacy is his son, Dr Prashant Dhannur, who envisioned Sangameshwar Hospital as a modern healthcare centre rooted in the same values. Under his leadership, the hospital was established to make quality healthcare more accessible for the people of Gadag and nearby areas, ensuring that patients no longer need to travel to bigger cities for trusted treatment.


Today, Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital stands as a complete medical shelter, offering expert care across a wide range of specialities. With advanced facilities, emergency support, and a dedicated team of professionals, the hospital continues to serve the community with care that is both modern and deeply rooted in compassion.

Our Core Values

At Sangameshwar Hospital, our values shape every interaction- guiding how we treat, heal, and serve our community.

Compassion

Patient care that goes beyond treatment — delivered with kindness and empathy.

Integrity

Ethical practices and honest communication at every stage of care.

Excellence

Clinical accuracy backed by modern facilities and continuous learning.

Accessibility

High-quality medical care that’s accessible and reasonably priced.

Our Vision

To carry forward Dr K. B Dhannur’s legacy and become a one-stop medical shelter for all health emergencies in Gadag- so that no patient needs to leave the city for quality care.

Our Mission

To make dependable, quality healthcare accessible to all at reasonable rates through clinical expertise, modern technology, and patient-first service.

A 100-bed multispeciality hospital in Gadag, built on over five decades of trusted care. We are committed to accessible, quality medical services delivered with compassion and integrity.
