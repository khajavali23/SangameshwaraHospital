ENT Specialist in Gadag- Complete Ear, Nose, and Throat Care at Sangameshwar Hospital

The Ear, Nose, and Throat (ENT) Department at Sangameshwar Hospital provides specialised care for all ENT-related conditions, from common infections to more complex disorders. Our experienced ENT specialists use advanced diagnostic tools and proven treatment methods to help patients recover quickly and comfortably. We treat patients of all ages from children with recurring ear infections to adults with sinus issues or hearing problems. Whether you need a simple ear cleaning, voice therapy, or treatment for chronic sinusitis, we ensure every patient receives personalised, evidence-based care.

Conditions We Treat

Our ENT specialists diagnose and manage a wide range of ear, nose, and throat conditions, including:

  • Ear infections and discharge
  • Hearing loss and tinnitus (ringing in the ears)
  • Balance disorders and vertigo
  • Sinusitis (acute and chronic)
  • Nasal blockages and allergies
  • Deviated nasal septum
  • Tonsillitis and adenoid problems
  • Sore throat and voice changes
  • Head and neck swellings
  • Sleep apnoea and snoring issues

Common Procedures & Services

Our ENT specialists provide a variety of diagnostic and therapeutic procedures to address ear, nose, and throat concerns:

  • Ear wax removal
  • Hearing tests (audiometry)
  • Nasal endoscopy
  • Throat endoscopy (laryngoscopy)
  • Treatment for nosebleeds
  • Sinus irrigation and care
  • Voice therapy
  • Allergy management
  • Minor ENT surgeries (tonsillectomy, adenoidectomy)
  • Foreign body removal from ear, nose, or throat

When to Visit an ENT Specialist

You should consult our ENT department if you experience:

  • Persistent ear pain or discharge
  • Reduced hearing or ringing in the ears
  • Frequent sore throats or hoarseness
  • Blocked nose or chronic sinus pain
  • Difficulty breathing through the nose
  • Recurring tonsil infections
  • Dizziness or balance problems
  • Snoring that affects sleep quality
