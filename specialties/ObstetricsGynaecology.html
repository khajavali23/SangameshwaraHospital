When to Visit an Obstetrician or Gynecologist

You should consult our Obstetrics & Gynaecology Department if you experience:

  • Irregular or painful periods
  • Unusual vaginal discharge or pelvic pain
  • Difficulty conceiving or planning a pregnancy
  • Symptoms of menopause (hot flashes, mood changes)
  • Abnormal uterine bleeding
  • Recurrent urinary tract infections
  • Need for routine gynecological exams or Pap smears
  • Pregnancy confirmation or antenatal care
