Compassionate Care for Every Stage of Women's Health

At Sangameshwar Hospital in Gadag, our Obstetrics & Gynaecology Department is dedicated to providing personalized care for women at every stage of life—from adolescence and pregnancy to menopause and beyond. Our experienced consultants and compassionate staff offer evidence-based treatments, ensuring comprehensive care for gynecological issues, pregnancy, and family planning with sensitivity and expertise.

Conditions We Treat

Our team manages a wide range of women’s health conditions, including:

  • Menstrual irregularities (PCOD, heavy bleeding, missed periods)
  • Pelvic pain and infections
  • Vaginal discharge and urinary tract infections (UTIs)
  • Fibroids and ovarian cysts
  • Menopause-related symptoms
  • Endometriosis
  • Hormonal imbalances

Common Procedures & Services

We offer a comprehensive range of obstetric and gynecological services:

  • Early pregnancy scans and routine antenatal check-ups
  • Nutritional guidance during pregnancy
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring
  • Fetal growth and well-being assessments
  • Contraceptive counseling and IUD insertion/removal
  • Cervical screening and Pap smears
  • Dilation and curettage (D&C)
  • Medical management for miscarriages
  • Colposcopy referrals

When to Visit an Obstetrician or Gynecologist

You should consult our Obstetrics & Gynaecology Department if you experience:

  • Irregular or painful periods
  • Unusual vaginal discharge or pelvic pain
  • Difficulty conceiving or planning a pregnancy
  • Symptoms of menopause (hot flashes, mood changes)
  • Abnormal uterine bleeding
  • Recurrent urinary tract infections
  • Need for routine gynecological exams or Pap smears
  • Pregnancy confirmation or antenatal care
