Infertility Specialist in Gadag – Expert Fertility Care at Sangameshwar Hospital

At Sangameshwar Hospital, we understand that the journey to parenthood can be filled with challenges and emotions. Our Infertility Department is dedicated to helping couples achieve their dream of having a child through evidence-based fertility treatments, personalised care, and emotional support. Led by experienced infertility specialists, we provide comprehensive evaluations for both partners to identify the root cause of infertility and recommend the most suitable treatment plan. Our goal is to offer world-class fertility care in Gadag, so couples do not have to travel to larger cities for treatment.

Conditions We Treat

We address a wide range of fertility-related concerns, including:

  • Irregular or absent ovulation
  • Polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS)
  • Blocked or damaged fallopian tubes
  • Low sperm count or motility
  • Unexplained infertility
  • Endometriosis-related infertility
  • Hormonal imbalances affecting fertility
  • Recurrent miscarriages
  • Age-related fertility decline

Common Procedures & Services

Our fertility services include:

  • Fertility assessment for both partners
  • Ovulation induction
  • Intrauterine insemination (IUI)
  • In vitro fertilisation (IVF) (in collaboration with advanced centres where required)
  • Hormonal therapy for reproductive health
  • Semen analysis and sperm preparation
  • Surgical correction for certain fertility issues (laparoscopy, hysteroscopy)
  • Counselling and emotional support for couples

Our Approach to Fertility Care

At Sangameshwar Hospital, fertility treatment is not just about medical procedures—it’s about compassionate guidance at every step. We follow a structured approach:

  • Detailed Evaluation: Assessing both partners to identify underlying causes.
  • Personalised Planning: Designing treatment plans based on medical history, age, and fertility goals.
  • Stepwise Treatment: Starting with simpler, less invasive options before moving to advanced techniques.
  • Emotional Support: Providing counselling to help couples cope with the stress of infertility.
  • Continuous Monitoring: Regular follow-ups to track progress and adapt treatment as needed.

Why Choose Sangameshwar Hospital for Infertility Treatment in Gadag?

Specialist expertise, complete evaluation, patient privacy, and holistic care—our fertility services are designed to provide both advanced medical treatment and compassionate support, right here in Gadag.

  • Specialist Expertise: Experienced infertility doctors offering advanced fertility solutions.
  • Complete Evaluation: Comprehensive tests for both male and female fertility factors.
  • Patient Privacy: Confidential consultations in a respectful, non-judgmental environment.
  • Accessible Care: Advanced fertility treatments available locally without long-distance travel.
  • Holistic Support: Medical treatment complemented by emotional counselling.
