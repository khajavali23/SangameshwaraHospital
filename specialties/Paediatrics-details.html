Comprehensive Pediatric Care at Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag

The Pediatrics Department at Sangameshwar Hospital in Gadag is dedicated to providing comprehensive medical care for newborns, infants, children, and adolescents. Our experienced pediatricians combine compassionate care with evidence-based practices to support your child’s healthy growth and development. From routine check-ups to managing complex childhood illnesses, we focus on early diagnosis, preventive care, and personalized treatment, ensuring high-quality pediatric care for families in Gadag and surrounding areas.

Conditions We Treat

We manage a wide range of childhood health conditions, including:

  • Common infections (cold, flu, ear infections, throat infections)
  • Fever and viral illnesses
  • Asthma and allergies
  • Nutritional deficiencies (anemia, vitamin deficiencies)
  • Growth and developmental delays
  • Skin conditions (eczema, rashes, fungal infections)
  • Gastrointestinal issues (diarrhea, constipation, vomiting)
  • Childhood obesity
  • Chronic conditions like diabetes in children
  • Neonatal health concerns

Common Procedures & Services

Our pediatric team offers a range of services to support your child’s health:

  • Routine health check-ups and vaccinations
  • Newborn care and screenings
  • Developmental assessments
  • Growth monitoring
  • Asthma management plans
  • Nutritional guidance for children
  • Treatment for minor injuries
  • School health assessments
  • Management of acute illnesses

When to Visit a Pediatrician

You should consult our Pediatrics Department if your child experiences:

  • Persistent fever or cough
  • Unusual tiredness or irritability
  • Breathing difficulties or wheezing
  • Delayed growth or developmental milestones
  • Frequent infections
  • Poor appetite or sudden weight changes
  • Skin rashes that do not improve
  • Digestive issues lasting more than a few days

Why Choose Sangameshwar Hospital for Pediatric Care?

Our Pediatrics Department is committed to providing exceptional care for children in a supportive environment. Here’s why we stand out:
