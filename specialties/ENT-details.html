Expert Ear, Nose, and Throat Care at Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag

At Sangameshwar Hospital in Gadag, our ENT Department provides specialized care for a wide range of ear, nose, and throat conditions, from common infections to complex disorders. Our experienced ENT specialists use advanced diagnostic tools and evidence-based treatments to ensure quick and comfortable recovery. We treat patients of all ages, from children with recurrent ear infections to adults with sinus issues or hearing problems, delivering personalized care tailored to your needs.

Conditions We Treat

Our ENT specialists diagnose and manage a wide range of ear, nose, and throat conditions, including:

  • Ear infections and discharge
  • Hearing loss and tinnitus (ringing in the ears)
  • Balance disorders and vertigo
  • Sinusitis (acute and chronic)
  • Nasal blockages and allergies
  • Deviated nasal septum
  • Tonsillitis and adenoid issues
  • Sore throat and voice disorders
  • Head and neck swellings
  • Sleep apnea and snoring issues

Common Procedures & Services

Our ENT specialists offer a variety of diagnostic and therapeutic procedures:

  • Ear wax removal
  • Hearing tests (audiometry)
  • Nasal endoscopy
  • Throat endoscopy (laryngoscopy)
  • Treatment for nosebleeds
  • Sinus irrigation and care
  • Voice therapy
  • Allergy management
  • Minor ENT surgeries (tonsillectomy, adenoidectomy)
  • Foreign body removal from ear, nose, or throat

When to Visit an ENT Specialist

You should consult our ENT Department if you experience:

  • Persistent ear pain or discharge
  • Reduced hearing or ringing in the ears
  • Frequent sore throats or hoarseness
  • Blocked nose or chronic sinus pain
  • Difficulty breathing through the nose
  • Recurrent tonsil infections
  • Dizziness or balance problems
  • Snoring that affects sleep quality
