Expert Urological Care at Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag

The Urology Department at Sangameshwar Hospital in Gadag provides comprehensive care for conditions affecting the urinary tract and male reproductive system. Backed by experienced consultants and advanced diagnostics, we offer timely and precise diagnosis, effective treatment, and personalized care for patients across all age groups. Whether you're facing a recurring urinary infection or a complex urological issue, our team delivers evidence-based medical management with compassion and discretion. Our department collaborates closely with specialties like general surgery and nephrology to ensure multidisciplinary care.

Conditions We Treat

We manage a wide range of urological conditions, including:

  • Urinary tract infections (UTIs)
  • Kidney stones
  • Enlarged prostate (benign prostatic hyperplasia - BPH)
  • Male infertility
  • Erectile dysfunction
  • Bladder control issues (incontinence)
  • Urethral strictures
  • Blood in urine (hematuria)
  • Painful urination or frequent urge to urinate
  • Scrotal swelling or testicular pain

Common Procedures & Services

Our urology team offers the following diagnostic and treatment services:

  • Urine analysis and culture
  • Ultrasound KUB (Kidney, Ureter, Bladder)
  • Uroflowmetry
  • Urethral dilation
  • Catheterization (urethral and suprapubic)
  • Cystoscopy (diagnostic)
  • Medical management for BPH and prostatitis
  • Medical treatment for kidney stones
  • Treatment for recurrent UTIs
  • STI screening and management
  • Counseling and treatment for male sexual dysfunction

When to Visit a Urologist

You should consult our Urology Department if you experience:

  • Burning sensation or pain during urination
  • Frequent or urgent need to urinate
  • Blood in urine or cloudy urine
  • Severe back or abdominal pain (possible kidney stones)
  • Difficulty urinating or weak urine flow
  • Incontinence or bladder control issues
  • Scrotal swelling or testicular pain
  • Concerns about male infertility or sexual dysfunction
