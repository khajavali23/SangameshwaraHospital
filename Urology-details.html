Expert Urology Care at Sangameshwara Hospital

Featured 4.9

At Sangameshwara Hospital in Guntur, our Urology department provides advanced care for conditions affecting the urinary tract and male reproductive system. Our expert urologists use state-of-the-art diagnostics and minimally invasive treatments to deliver personalized, compassionate care, ensuring optimal health and comfort for our patients.

Our specialists offer comprehensive treatments for kidney stones, prostate conditions, bladder issues, and more, using advanced techniques like laser lithotripsy and robotic surgery to ensure effective outcomes with minimal recovery time.

Addressing Urological Conditions

Urological conditions, such as kidney stones, prostate issues, or urinary incontinence, can significantly impact quality of life. At Sangameshwara Hospital, our Urology team provides advanced diagnostics, minimally invasive treatments, and tailored care plans to address these issues, ensuring early intervention and lasting relief.

Kidney stones are treated with methods like laser lithotripsy, ureteroscopy, or minimally invasive surgery, depending on stone size and location, for effective removal and relief.

Contact Sangameshwara Hospital at +91 91108 86612 or book online via our appointment page to schedule a consultation with our urology specialists.

Prostate surgery, such as TURP or robotic prostatectomy, treats conditions like benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) or prostate cancer, with minimally invasive options for faster recovery.

Urinary incontinence is managed with lifestyle changes, pelvic floor exercises, medications, or surgery, tailored to the patient’s condition by our expert urologists.

Urological diagnostics include ultrasound, cystoscopy, urodynamic testing, and blood tests to accurately diagnose conditions affecting the urinary and reproductive systems.
