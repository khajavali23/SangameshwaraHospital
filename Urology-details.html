Expert Urological Care at Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag

The Urology Department at Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, provides comprehensive care for conditions related to the urinary tract and male reproductive system. Backed by experienced consultants and advanced diagnostics, we aim to offer timely and precise diagnosis, effective treatment, and personalised care for patients across all age groups. Whether you're facing a recurring urinary infection or a more complex urological issue, our team focuses on delivering evidence-based medical management with compassion and discretion. Our department also works closely with other specialities like general surgery when required, ensuring complete, multidisciplinary care.

Conditions Treated

We manage a wide range of urological concerns, including:

  • Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs)
  • Kidney stones
  • Enlarged prostate (BPH)
  • Male infertility
  • Erectile dysfunction
  • Bladder control issues (incontinence)
  • Urethral strictures
  • Blood in urine (haematuria)
  • Painful urination or frequent urge to urinate
  • Scrotal swelling or testicular pain

Common Procedures & Services

Depending on the condition, we offer the following diagnostic and treatment services:

  • Urine analysis and culture
  • Ultrasound KUB (Kidney, Ureter, Bladder)
  • Uroflowmetry
  • Urethral dilation
  • Catheterisation (urethral and suprapubic)
  • Cystoscopy (diagnostic)
  • Medical management for BPH and prostatitis
  • Medical treatment for kidney stones
  • Treatment for recurrent UTIs
  • STI screening and management
  • Counselling and treatment for male sexual dysfunction

Why Choose Sangameshwar Hospital for Urology?

Our urology department offers:

  • Experienced urology consultants
  • Privacy-focused and compassionate care
  • Accurate diagnostics with modern equipment
  • Integrated care with internal medicine and nephrology
  • Transparent guidance and referrals when surgery is needed
