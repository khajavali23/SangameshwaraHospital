Compassionate Care Through Every Stage of Womanhood

At Sangameshwar Hospital, we understand that women’s health needs are unique, evolving with each phase of life from adolescence and pregnancy to menopause and beyond. Our Obstetrics & Gynaecology department is dedicated to providing personalised care that is both professional and compassionate. Whether you're planning a family, navigating pregnancy, or managing gynaecological issues, our experienced doctors and support staff are here to guide you with sensitivity and expertise.

Our Services Include:

General Gynaecology

  • Menstrual irregularities (PCOD, heavy bleeding, missed periods)
  • Pelvic pain and infections
  • Vaginal discharge and UTIs
  • Fibroids and ovarian cysts
  • Menopause-related care

Pregnancy & Antenatal Care

  • Early pregnancy scans
  • Routine antenatal check-ups
  • Nutritional guidance during pregnancy
  • High-risk pregnancy monitoring
  • Foetal growth and well-being assessments

Family Planning & Contraception

  • Contraceptive counselling
  • Copper-T / IUD insertion and removal
  • Emergency contraception
  • Pre-pregnancy health checks

Daycare & Minor Procedures

  • D&C (Dilation and Curettage)
  • Cervical screening and Pap smears
  • Colposcopy referrals.
  • Medical management for miscarriages.

Why Choose Sangameshwar Hospital for Women’s Health?

At Sangameshwar Hospital, women’s health is treated with the expertise it deserves and the compassion it needs. Our Obstetrics & Gynaecology department is led by experienced consultants who provide personalised care through every stage of life – from adolescence to pregnancy, and beyond.

  • Experienced consultants for obstetrics and gynaecology
  • Personalised care for women of all ages
  • Expert management of high-risk pregnancies
  • Advanced diagnostic and treatment facilities
  • Comprehensive care for complex gynaecological conditions
  • Supportive, patient-centred approach
  • Evidence-based treatments in a safe environment
