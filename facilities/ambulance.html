Ambulance Facility at Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

Medical emergencies can occur without warning, and in such moments, every second counts. At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, we understand the importance of providing timely, reliable, and well-equipped ambulance services. Our facility ensures that patients in Gadag and the surrounding areas have round-the-clock access to emergency transportation, enabling them to receive immediate medical attention at our hospital. Whether it’s a cardiac arrest, road traffic accident, stroke, trauma, or critical illness, our ambulance facility is designed to bridge the gap between the site of emergency and expert hospital care.

Fully Equipped and Advanced Ambulance

Our ambulance is not just a vehicle but a mobile intensive care unit. The ambulance is fitted with modern equipment to provide pre-hospital emergency care:

  • Oxygen supply, ventilator, and airway management support
  • Cardiac monitors and defibrillators for heart emergencies
  • Infusion pumps and life-saving drugs for critical patients
  • Stretcher and immobilisation equipment for trauma cases
  • Continuous communication with our Emergency and ICU departments

This ensures that patients remain under medical supervision throughout their journey, minimising risks and improving survival rates.

Skilled Paramedics and Trained Emergency Staff

An ambulance is only as effective as the team managing it. At Sangameshwar Hospital, our ambulance is staffed with trained paramedics, emergency technicians, and nursing staff who are well-versed in life-saving procedures. They work in close coordination with our emergency medicine doctors, ensuring a smooth handover of patients upon arrival at the hospital.

Coverage Across Gadag and Neighbouring Regions

Being a trusted healthcare provider in Gadag, our ambulance services are available not just within the city limits but also extend to nearby taluks and rural areas. This wide coverage makes advanced medical care accessible to people in remote locations who may not have immediate hospital facilities nearby. Our quick response system ensures that patients can be transported swiftly, reducing delays in critical treatment.

Patient-Centric Emergency Care

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, our focus is always on patient safety, comfort, and quick response. From the moment a call is received, our emergency team coordinates to dispatch an ambulance immediately. The patient’s condition is continuously monitored en route, and doctors at the hospital are alerted in advance to ensure that treatment begins the moment the patient arrives.

Why Choose Sangameshwar Hospital’s Ambulance Facility in Gadag?

With our dedicated ambulance facility, Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, stands committed to providing fast, reliable, and life-saving emergency services to the community.

  • 24/7 availability, including nights, weekends, and holidays
  • Advanced life-support systems for critically ill patients
  • Expert paramedics and nursing staff trained in emergency response
  • Swift coverage across Gadag and nearby rural areas
  • Seamless coordination with hospital Emergency & ICU departments

A 100-bed multispeciality hospital in Gadag, built on over five decades of trusted care. We are committed to accessible, quality medical services delivered with compassion and integrity.
