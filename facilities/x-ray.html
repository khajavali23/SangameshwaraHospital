X-Ray Facility in Gadag - Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, we believe that timely and accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective treatment. To support this, we have a modern X-Ray facility within our hospital that provides quick and reliable imaging services for patients of all age groups.

What is an X-Ray?

An X-Ray is a painless and non-invasive imaging test that helps doctors look inside the body to detect injuries, fractures, or health conditions. It uses a safe and controlled amount of radiation to create images of bones, joints, and certain internal organs. X-Rays are often the first step in diagnosis for many medical concerns.

X-Ray Services at Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

Our hospital offers a wide range of X-Ray services, including:

  • Chest X-Rays: To check for lung infections, pneumonia, TB, or heart conditions.
  • Abdominal X-Rays: To detect blockages, stones, or digestive issues.
  • Bone and Joint X-Rays: For fractures, dislocations, arthritis, and bone health.
  • Spine X-Rays: To identify spinal injuries, deformities, or disc problems.
  • Specialised X-Rays: As advised by doctors for dental, ENT, or orthopaedic concerns.

Advantages of Our X-Ray Facility in Gadag

Digital Imaging: Our advanced digital X-Ray system provides clear and accurate results, ensuring better diagnosis compared to traditional methods.

Quick Results: Reports are generated promptly, helping doctors plan treatment without delay.

Safe Procedure: We follow strict safety protocols and use low radiation doses, making the process safe even for children and elderly patients.

Convenient Access: As the X-Ray unit is located within the hospital, patients do not need to travel elsewhere for diagnostic imaging.
