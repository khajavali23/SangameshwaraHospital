Intensive Care Unit (ICU) in Gadag - Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, our Intensive Care Unit (ICU) is designed to provide advanced, round-the-clock critical care for patients with life-threatening conditions. Equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by a highly skilled team of intensivists, nurses, and support staff, our ICU ensures comprehensive monitoring, timely interventions, and compassionate support. We cater to patients requiring intensive treatment for severe injuries, post-surgical complications, respiratory failure, cardiac emergencies, and other critical illnesses, delivering personalized care to stabilize and improve patient outcomes.

Services Provided in Our ICU

Our ICU offers a wide range of critical care services, including:

  • Ventilator support for respiratory distress.
  • Cardiac monitoring and emergency interventions.
  • Management of post-operative complications.
  • Treatment for severe infections and sepsis.
  • Trauma and multi-organ failure care.
  • Advanced life support and resuscitation.

Why Choose Our ICU at Sangameshwar Hospital?

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Modern ICU equipped with cutting-edge monitoring systems and life-saving equipment.

Expert Medical Team: 24/7 availability of intensivists and critical care specialists.

Patient-Centered Care: Compassionate support for patients and their families during critical times.

Integrated Approach: Seamless coordination with other departments for holistic treatment.

Emergency Readiness: Rapid response team for immediate critical care needs.

Our Commitment

Guided by the legacy of Dr. Dhannur and the vision of Dr. Prashant Dhannur, our ICU at Sangameshwar Hospital is committed to providing world-class critical care in Gadag. We strive to offer timely, effective, and affordable treatment to save lives and support recovery, ensuring peace of mind for families in times of crisis.

A 100-bed multispeciality hospital in Gadag, built on over five decades of trusted care. We are committed to accessible, quality medical services delivered with compassion and integrity.
