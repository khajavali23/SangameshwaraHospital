Advanced Operation Theatres in Gadag – Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, we believe that safe surgeries require not only expert doctors but also world-class infrastructure. To support this vision, our hospital is equipped with three advanced Operation Theatres (OTs) that meet high standards of safety, hygiene, and technology. These facilities are designed to handle both routine and complex surgeries, ensuring that the people of Gadag and nearby regions do not have to travel to bigger cities for quality surgical care.

Modular Operation Theatres in Gadag

Out of the three, two are state-of-the-art Modular Operation Theatres. Modular OTs are considered the gold standard in modern surgery because they are built with specialised sterile panels, advanced air filtration systems, and laminar airflow technology that reduce the risk of infections. At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, our modular OTs are designed to support a wide range of specialties including:

  • General and Laparoscopic Surgery
  • Orthopaedic Procedures
  • Gynaecology and Obstetrics Surgeries
  • Urology and ENT Surgeries

Specialised Emergency Operation Theatre

In addition to modular OTs, we have a dedicated Emergency Operation Theatre in Gadag that remains ready round the clock. Whether it is a road accident, sudden appendicitis, internal bleeding, or any other life-threatening condition, this specialised OT ensures that patients receive immediate surgical care without losing critical time. The emergency OT is located strategically close to the casualty and intensive care units, allowing smooth patient transfer and faster surgical intervention. Our team of skilled surgeons, anaesthetists, and nurses are trained to handle emergencies with efficiency and compassion.

Why Choose Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital for Surgery in Gadag?

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Two modular OTs designed as per international standards.

24/7 Emergency Care: A fully functional emergency OT for critical situations.

Expert Surgical Team: Experienced doctors supported by trained nursing and technical staff.

Patient Safety First: Strict infection control, advanced monitoring systems, and sterile protocols.

By combining advanced facilities with the healing legacy of Dr. Dhannur, Sangameshwar Hospital ensures that people of Gadag and neighbouring towns receive the best surgical care at reasonable costs, closer to home.

A 100-bed multispeciality hospital in Gadag, built on over five decades of trusted care. We are committed to accessible, quality medical services delivered with compassion and integrity.
