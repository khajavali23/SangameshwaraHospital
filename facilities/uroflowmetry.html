Uroflowmetry Test in Gadag- Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, we offer advanced diagnostic facilities to help identify and treat urological conditions effectively. One of the key diagnostic tools available at our hospital is the Uroflowmetry test- a simple and non-invasive test that measures how well urine flows from the bladder.

What is Uroflowmetry?

Uroflowmetry is a test that records the speed and volume of urine flow during urination. It helps doctors understand how well the bladder and urethra are functioning. By identifying abnormalities in the flow pattern, the test assists in diagnosing problems related to the urinary tract.

When is Uroflowmetry Recommended?

Doctors may suggest a uroflowmetry test in Gadag for patients experiencing:

  • Difficulty starting or stopping urination
  • Weak or interrupted urine stream
  • Frequent urge to urinate
  • Painful urination
  • Incomplete emptying of the bladder
  • Conditions such as prostate enlargement, urethral stricture, or bladder dysfunction

How is the Test Done?

The uroflowmetry test is very simple and does not require any invasive procedures. Patients are asked to urinate into a special device called a uroflowmeter. The machine records:

  • The speed of urine flow
  • The amount of urine passed
  • The time taken to empty the bladder

The results are then analysed by our urologists to check for any irregularities.

Benefits of Uroflowmetry at Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

Non-invasive & Painless: No needles, cuts, or discomfort involved.

Quick & Reliable: The test takes only a few minutes and gives immediate results.

Early Diagnosis: Helps detect urinary problems before they become severe.

Accurate Assessment: Provides valuable information for planning further treatment or surgery.
