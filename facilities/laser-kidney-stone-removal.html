Laser Facility for Kidney Stone Removal at Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, Gadag, we provide state-of-the-art laser treatment for kidney stone removal, ensuring patients receive safe, precise, and minimally invasive care. Kidney stones can cause severe pain, urinary obstruction, and recurring infections if not treated on time.

Why Choose Laser Treatment for Kidney Stones?

Laser treatment, also known as laser lithotripsy, is one of the most effective and widely recommended methods for managing kidney stones. Compared to traditional surgery, it offers several benefits:

  • Minimally invasive – No large incisions are required.
  • High precision – Targets only the stone without harming nearby tissues.
  • Quick recovery – Patients can usually resume normal activities within a short time.
  • Suitable for complex stones – Works even for hard-to-reach or multiple stones.

Our Expertise in Urology Care

Our team of experienced urologists in Gadag specialises in treating kidney stones using modern technology. With years of expertise in endourology and laser procedures, we ensure that patients receive the highest quality care with minimal discomfort.

When Should You Consider Laser Stone Removal?

You may need laser kidney stone removal if:

  • Stones are larger than 6 mm and cannot pass naturally.
  • You experience repeated urinary tract infections due to stones.
  • Stones are blocking urine flow, causing pain or kidney damage.
  • Other treatments, such as medications or shock wave therapy, have failed.

Comprehensive Urology Services

Along with laser stone removal, Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital also provides a complete range of urological services, including:

  • Endoscopy for urinary tract evaluation
  • Uroflowmetry for bladder function testing
  • Minimally invasive procedures for prostate and urinary conditions
