Dialysis Centre at Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

Dialysis is a life-saving treatment for patients whose kidneys are unable to filter waste and excess fluid from the blood. At Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital in Gadag, we have established a modern dialysis unit dedicated to ensuring safe, effective, and comfortable treatment for patients with kidney conditions. Our team follows international standards of care, with special attention to hygiene, patient comfort, and continuous monitoring during every dialysis session.

State-of-the-Art Dialysis Facilities

Our hospital is equipped with advanced dialysis machines, sterile environments, and strict infection-control protocols to provide uninterrupted and safe treatment. Every dialysis procedure is designed to give patients a stress-free experience with comfortable seating, clean surroundings, and round-the-clock nursing support.

  • Safe and sterile care with regular machine disinfection and single-use consumables
  • 24x7 emergency readiness to handle complications, if any
  • Personalised attention so that every patient feels cared for throughout the process

Patient Safety & Comfort First

At Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, we understand that dialysis is not just a procedure but an essential part of our patients’ lives. Our unit is built to reduce waiting times, ensure on-time sessions, and provide constant supervision by trained dialysis staff. We maintain stringent infection-control practices to minimise the risk of cross-infection, giving families confidence in the quality of care provided.

Why Choose Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital for Dialysis in Gadag?

  • Modern and well-maintained dialysis equipment
  • Highly trained nursing and technical staff
  • A patient-friendly atmosphere with continuous monitoring
  • Affordable treatment options without compromising on quality
  • Convenient location in the heart of Gadag, making it accessible for regular sessions

Trusted Dialysis Care in Gadag

Patients across Gadag and neighbouring districts trust us for dialysis care because of our commitment to quality, safety, and accessibility. Our dialysis centre is designed to provide a supportive environment, ensuring patients receive the best care possible during their treatment journey.

A 100-bed multispeciality hospital in Gadag, built on over five decades of trusted care. We are committed to accessible, quality medical services delivered with compassion and integrity.
