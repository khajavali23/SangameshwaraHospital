Pharmacy at Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital

The pharmacy at Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital ensures that every patient has easy and timely access to genuine, prescribed medications. Conveniently located within the hospital premises, our pharmacy is designed to support patients, attendants, and walk-in customers with safe, reliable, and affordable medicines.

Assured Quality & Authenticity

We source all medicines and healthcare products directly from trusted manufacturers and authorised distributors. Every product is stored under recommended conditions to maintain its safety, potency, and effectiveness. This guarantees that patients receive only genuine and high-quality medicines.

Comprehensive Range of Medicines

Our pharmacy stocks a wide selection of:

  • Prescription medicines across all specialties
  • Over-the-counter (OTC) drugs
  • Surgical supplies and consumables
  • Nutritional supplements
  • Baby care and personal care essentials

This wide range makes our pharmacy a one-stop solution for patient needs.

Patient Convenience First

Immediate Access: Prescriptions from doctors within the hospital can be filled instantly.
Supportive Guidance: Our trained pharmacists are available to explain dosage instructions and answer patient queries.

Why Choose Our Pharmacy?

100% authentic, quality-tested medicines

Convenient location within the hospital premises

Patient-focused service with trained pharmacists

Affordable pricing and transparent billing

At Dr. Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, our pharmacy is not just a dispensing counter-it is an integral part of patient care.

A 100-bed multispeciality hospital in Gadag, built on over five decades of trusted care. We are committed to accessible, quality medical services delivered with compassion and integrity.
