About us

A Complete Shelter for All Your Health Needs

At Dr Dhannur’s Sangameshwar Hospital, we believe quality healthcare should be accessible, dependable, and close to home. Built on the legacy of Dr K.B. Dhannur’s five decades of trusted service, the hospital has grown into a 100-bed multispeciality centre. Today, it stands as a one-stop destination for comprehensive medical care for the people of Gadag and nearby regions.

  • 100-Bed Multispeciality Hospital
  • 50+ Years of Trusted Medical Legacy
  • Advanced Facilities with Critical Care Support
  • Quality Medical Care, Made Reasonable and Reliable
