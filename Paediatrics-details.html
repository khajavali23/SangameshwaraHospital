Paediatric Hospital in Gadag- Comprehensive Child Care at Sangameshwar Hospital

TAt Sangameshwar Hospital, our Paediatrics Department is dedicated to providing complete medical care for newborns, infants, children, and adolescents. Led by experienced paediatricians, we combine compassionate care with up-to-date medical practices to ensure your child’s healthy growth and development. From routine check-ups to managing complex childhood illnesses, we focus on early diagnosis, preventive care, and personalised treatment. Our aim is to offer the children of Gadag and nearby areas the same quality of paediatric care available in bigger cities- right here, close to home.

Conditions We Treat

We manage a wide range of childhood health concerns, including:

  • Common infections (cold, flu, ear infections, throat infections)
  • Fever and viral illnesses
  • Asthma and allergies
  • Nutritional deficiencies (anaemia, vitamin deficiencies)
  • Growth and developmental delays
  • Skin conditions (eczema, rashes, fungal infections)
  • Gastrointestinal problems (diarrhoea, constipation, vomiting)
  • Childhood obesity
  • Chronic conditions like diabetes in children
  • Neonatal health issues

Common Procedures & Services

Our Paediatrics Department offers:

  • Routine health check-ups and vaccinations
  • Newborn care and monitoring
  • Developmental assessments
  • Growth tracking
  • Asthma management plans
  • Dietary guidance for children
  • Minor injury treatment
  • School health assessments

When to Visit a Paediatrician

You should seek medical attention for your child if you notice:

  • Persistent fever or cough
  • Unusual tiredness or irritability
  • Breathing difficulties or wheezing
  • Delayed growth or developmental milestones
  • Frequent infections
  • Poor appetite or sudden weight changes
  • Skin rashes that do not improve
  • Digestive issues lasting more than a few days
