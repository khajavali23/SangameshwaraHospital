Expert Paediatric Care at Sangameshwara Hospital

Featured 4.9

At Sangameshwara Hospital in Guntur, our Paediatrics department provides compassionate, child-friendly care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our expert pediatricians use advanced diagnostics and gentle treatments to ensure the health and well-being of your child in a supportive environment.

Our specialists offer comprehensive services, including newborn care, vaccinations, and developmental assessments, using child-focused approaches to promote healthy growth and development.

  • Newborn Care
  • Vaccinations
  • Developmental Assessments
  • Pediatric Surgery
  • Asthma Management
  • Nutritional Counseling

Addressing Children’s Health Needs

Children’s health requires specialized, gentle care tailored to their unique needs. At Sangameshwara Hospital, our Paediatrics team provides advanced diagnostics, preventive care, and treatments to support healthy development, ensuring comfort for both children and their families.

Recommended vaccinations include MMR, DTaP, polio, and hepatitis vaccines, administered per national guidelines to protect against serious diseases.

Contact Sangameshwara Hospital at +91 91108 86612 or book online via our appointment page to schedule a consultation with our paediatric specialists.

Pediatric surgery addresses conditions like hernias or congenital anomalies in children, using minimally invasive techniques for faster recovery.

Asthma in children is managed with inhalers, medications, and lifestyle adjustments, tailored by our pediatricians to ensure effective control.

Developmental assessments evaluate a child’s growth, behavior, and milestones to identify and address any developmental delays early.
